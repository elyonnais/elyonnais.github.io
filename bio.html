About Me

Emily Lyonnais is a senior at Eastern Washington University. Currently working towards getting her BFA in Visual Communication Design, she has been busy taking art and design classes, interning, and working on her senior project.

When she finds free time, she enjoys attending concerts, listening to podcasts, spending time with her friends and family, and, obviously, creating some art. In terms of art making, she enjoys printmaking, drawing, and painting. She also has a growing fondness for photography.

All in all, she looks forward to growing in her skills as a designer and problem solver while being able to employ her artistic side.
