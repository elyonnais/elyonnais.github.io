About Me

Emily Lyonnais is a senior at Eastern Washington University. Currently pursuing her BFA in Visual Communication Design, she has been busy taking art and design classes, interning, and working on her senior project.

Whenever she has the chance, she appreciates attending concerts, spending time with her friends and family, and creating more art. In terms of art making, she enjoys printmaking, drawing, and painting. She also has a growing fondness for photography.

Emily continues to look forward to expanding her skills as a designer and problem solver while being able to employ her artistic side. She is thankful for all opportunities that come her way.
